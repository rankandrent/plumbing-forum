How to Fix a Leaking Pipe Under the Sink

Posted by John Smith
December 10, 2024
If you've discovered a leaking pipe under your sink, don't panic! This guide will help you fix it step by step.

Tools You'll Need:

  • Adjustable wrench
  • Pipe wrench
  • Plumber's tape
  • Bucket or large container
  • Towels or rags

Step 1: Locate the Leak

First, identify exactly where the leak is coming from. Common spots include:

  • Pipe joints
  • P-trap connections
  • Drain flange
  • Supply line connections

Step 2: Prepare the Area

Before starting any repairs:

  • Turn off the water supply
  • Place a bucket under the pipe to catch any water
  • Clear the area under the sink

Step 3: Fix the Leak

Depending on the type of leak:

  • For loose connections: Tighten with a wrench
  • For worn-out washers: Replace them
  • For cracked pipes: Replace the section
Remember to always wrap pipe threads with plumber's tape before reassembly!
